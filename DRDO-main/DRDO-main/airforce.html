AIR-FORCE

This is the history of DRDO-DesiDoc and How we have come along a long journey.

The Indian Air Force (IAF) is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. Its complement of personnel and aircraft assets ranks as the third most powerful air force in the world. Its primary mission is to secure Indian airspace and to conduct aerial warfare during armed conflicts

The IAF traces its lineage to the Royal Indian Air Force (RIAF), which was formed on 8 October 1932. The RIAF was renamed as the Royal Indian Air Force (RIAF) on 1 April 1933. The prefix "Royal" was dropped on 1 July 1947 when the RIAF became the Indian Air Force (IAF).





DRDO-DesiDoc

The Defence Scientific Information & Documentation Centre is a division of the Defence Research and Development Organisation. Located in Delhi, its main function is the collection, processing and dissemination of relevant technical information for DRDO scientists.
